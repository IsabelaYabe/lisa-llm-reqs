1.1	There shall be two levels of access; one for teachers and assistants and one for administrators.
1.2	The teachers and assistants shall only be allowed to enter or edit child comments and child arrival or departure times.
1.3	The administrators shall be allowed to enter or edit all data.
1.4	Only the administrators shall be allowed to view or print reports.
1.5	Only the administrators shall be allowed to view or print invoices.
1.6	All employees shall be allowed to use the Daily Reminders.
2.7	Each user shall be required to log on with a unique user name and password before using the system. The password does not need to be unique.
2.8	The user name shall be the first four charters of the employee's last name followed by the first three characters of their first name.
2.9	A password shall be assigned to each user.
2.10	The password shall contain 6 to 8 alphanumeric characters.
2.11	After three unsuccessful attempts to enter a password the user shall be locked out of the system until their password is reset.
2.12	A new password shall be assigned if a user forgets.
2.13	If there is no activity for 10 minutes the user shall be logged off of the system.
3.14	Users shall only be allowed to set daily reminders for themselves.
3.15	The date of the reminder shall be selected from a calendar.
3.16	The user shall be allowed to enter up to a 50 character message for their reminder.
3.17	When the user logs onto the system on the date of their reminder, they shall receive a popup window displaying their reminder message.
4.18	A child's record shall contain the dates that their required immunizations were received.
4.19	When a child is within two weeks of a required immunization a message shall be displayed when their teacher logs onto the system.
4.20	A child's record shall contain the date of their last physical examination.
4.21	The Administrators shall be able to enter the type and schedule of required immunizations.
4.22	A notification for required immunizations shall be printed on the monthly invoice.
5.23	If only a single child is enrolled a parent shall be billed $90 at the end of the each month.
5.24	If only two children are enrolled a parent shall be billed $157.50 at the end of each month.
5.25	If more than two children are enrolled a parent shall be billed $157.50 for the first two children plus $45 for each additional child at the end of each month.
5.26	An account history for each customer shall contain all activity by date.
5.27	Itemized customer invoices showing amount due shall be available for printing.
5.28	Customers shall be billed $10 an hour (per child) for late pick ups.
5.29	All invoices shall be available for printing at the end of each month.
6.30	A Customer Directory Report shall list the customers alphabetically by last name and include their address and phone number.
6.31	A Daily Classroom & Center Enrollment Report shall alphabetically list the names of children by classroom and indicate if they are present or absent.
6.32	A Birthday Lists & Child Age Report shall list the children alphabetically by last name and include their birthday and age.
6.33	A Parent/Child Cross Reference Report shall list the parents alphabetically by last name and include their children's first names.
6.34	A Family Registration Information Report shall list the customer names alphabetically and include their address and home telephone number.
6.35	An Immunization Due & History Report shall list the children alphabetically by last name and include any remaining immunizations that are required and the dates of previous immunizations.
6.36	The Child Enrollment/Withdrawal Report shall list the children alphabetically by last name and whether or not they are registered or withdrawn from the Child Care Center.
6.37	The Child Notes & Comments Report shall list the children alphabetically by last name and include any notes that are attached to their records.
6.38	The Account Activity Report shall list a selected customer's account activity by date.
7.39	When a user edits an account their user ID along with the date, time and a description of the change shall be recorded in the database.
7.42	The software shall support the ability to enter, store, and update parent, child and billing information.
7.43	Customer log notes shall store the date and comments regarding any correspondence with a parent.
8.44	Each classroom shall only accept a maximum of 20 children.
8.45	Each student shall only belong to one class
8.46	One teacher and one assistant shall be assigned to each class.
8.47	A waiting list shall store the names and phone numbers of up to 100 potential customers waiting for a classroom opening to enroll their child.
9.48	The system shall have a Web based interface that works with Internet Explorer and Netscape Navigator.
9.49	The background color of all windows shall be blue.
9.50	The system shall respond to all user requests within 20 seconds.
